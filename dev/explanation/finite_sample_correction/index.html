Finite-sample Correction

We follow the convention used in Angelopoulos and Bates (2021) and Barber et al. (2021) to correct for the finite-sample bias of the empirical quantile. Specifically, we use the following definition of the (1âˆ’Î±) empirical quantile:

\[\hat{q}_{n,\alpha}^{+}\{v\} = \frac{\lceil (n+1)(1-\alpha)\rceil}{n}\]

\[\hat{q}_{n,\alpha}^{-}\{v\} = \frac{\lfloor (n+1)\alpha \rfloor}{n} = - \hat{q}_{n,\alpha}^{+}\{-v\}\]
